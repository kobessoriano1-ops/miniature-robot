Get to Know Me: My Personal Story

Hey, I'm Kobe, the creator I'm 17 years old, and my passion lies in bringing ideas to life through digital mediums. I specialize in website development, graphic design, and video editing. Beyond technical skills, I enjoy helping people brainstorm and create fun, engaging titles and concepts for videos.

I'm a highly organized person and I often find myself organizing my personal schedules and even my email inbox just for fun! This attention to detail extends to my work. I'm also proficient in public speaking, essay writing, and managing complex tasks effectively.

Growing up in a financially struggling family wasn't a setback; I see it as a stepping stone to a future milestone. This experience made me resilient, more creative, and intensely curious about the world and how things work. It's the fuel for my constant learning.

Outside of creating, my hobbies include reading manhwa, spending time with friends, and simply enjoying life!
